## Q2 Highlights

The team shipped **23 of 27 planned milestones** this quarter — a 7% improvement over Q1.

Standout deliveries:
- **Terminus v2 beta** launched to 400 early-access customers with a 94% satisfaction score
- **Zero-downtime deploys** rolled out across all production services, cutting deploy-related incidents by 60%
- **Design system v2** token migration completed; all product surfaces now consistent

Areas to improve:
- Mobile performance work slipped two sprints; root cause was under-estimated scope on the image pipeline
- Incident response SLA missed twice in June — post-mortems and revised runbooks are in review

## Q3 Focus Areas

1. **Terminus v2 GA** — general availability to all customers by end of July
2. **Observability uplift** — distributed tracing across all services; target: p95 latency dashboards live by August
3. **Developer experience** — local dev setup time from 45 min → under 5 min; new contributor guide
4. **Mobile performance** — carry-over from Q2; target Lighthouse score ≥ 90 on all core flows
